Visit Isfahan

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Isfahan.

Top three activities to do at Isfahan

  • Attend a show or a concert

    The Isfahan Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

  • Go for a picnic in the local park

    The Isfahan Park is perfect for picnic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

  • Play sports at the Lilleborg field

    Play volleyball, football, basketball, or lift some weights.
